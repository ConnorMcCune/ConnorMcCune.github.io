About Abiotic Factor

Abiotic Factor is a cooperative survival-crafting game set inside a massive underground research facility where scientific experimentation has gone terribly wrong. Players take on the role of scientists forced to survive after containment breaches unleash hostile entities, unstable environments, and dangerous anomalies. Unlike traditional survival games, Abiotic Factor emphasizes science-driven progression. Crafting advanced tools, understanding systems, and working together are essential for survival.

The World

The facility is divided into distinct sectors, each with its own hazards, resources, and environmental storytelling. From abandoned labs to overgrown containment zones, every area reflects the consequences of unchecked experimentation. The deeper you explore, the more secrets the facility reveals — not all of them meant to be uncovered.

Core Gameplay

  • Co-op Survival – Play with friends and rely on teamwork to overcome threats
  • Crafting & Research – Build tools, weapons, and machines using scientific principles
  • Environmental Hazards – Radiation, anomalies, hostile creatures, and system failures
  • Exploration – Discover lore, hidden rooms, and experimental technologies

Abiotic Factor rewards curiosity, preparation, and collaboration.

Inspiration

Abiotic Factor draws inspiration from classic science-fiction themes: containment failures, ethical boundaries of research, and the consequences of curiosity without restraint.
